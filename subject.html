Where it began

  • It began as version 1.6. This was the basic idea of the game, it was very popular among European players and quickly spread as it gained traction. After 1.6, the full game came out, this included more maps, guns and looked a lot better. Keep in mind, this game came out in 1999, so there was only so much they could do at that time.

How it became known

  • It became know through various local tournaments, which evolved into even bigger tournaments involving huge amounts money and attendance. It was popular because of the nuances it incorporated, it was an industrial revolution for the gaming community. It established a new genre of video game. Copies of Counter-Strike, such as Valorant, would not even be a thing. In January, after 22 years since 1.6, it averaged 81,000 viewers on Twitch.
